About Indo Route Export

Rooted in Gujarat's spice heartland, exporting to the world.

Indo Route Export is a vertically integrated spice company based in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. From our own 50-acre farms and trusted grower network, we process and export premium turmeric, chili, cumin, coriander, and more to buyers across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and Africa.
