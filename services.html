Our Farms & Grower Network

We don't just trade spices — we grow them. Our 50+ acres of farmland in Gujarat give us direct control over quality from seed to harvest. Beyond our own farms, we maintain a vetted network of trusted growers across Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, and Karnataka.

This vertical integration means consistent quality, reliable supply year-round, and pricing advantages that get passed directly to our buyers.

Certifications Explained

What our certifications mean for you.

🏛

MSME Registration

Registered under India's Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises. This means we're a verified, government-recognized business entity — giving you confidence in our legitimacy and compliance as a trade partner.

🌿

APEDA Certification

Certified by the Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority. This authorizes us to officially export agricultural products from India and ensures our operations meet national export quality benchmarks.

🛡

FSSAI License

Licensed under the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. Every product we export meets India's stringent food safety regulations — covering hygiene, contamination limits, labelling, and traceability requirements.
